Mission

Empowering communities to reclaim food sovereignty through sustainable practices, environmental education, and the protection of pollinators and ecosystems.

Vision

A future where food is a right, not a privilege—where communities reclaim land, restore ecosystems, and resist systems that keep them hungry and powerless.

The 8 Pillars of Rooted Communities

  • Food Justice: We believe access to healthy, free, and culturally appropriate food is a human right—not a privilege.
  • Community Empowerment: We center the voices, leadership, and strength of communities to reclaim their power over food and land.
  • Sustainability: We promote regenerative practices that protect soil, water, pollinators, and ecosystems for future generations.
  • Environmental Protection: We protect and restore the natural world as an act of resistance and respect—recognizing healthy ecosystems as essential to food sovereignty and community survival.
  • Education and Accessibility: We provide inclusive, hands-on education that equips all people with the skills and knowledge to grow and sustain their own food.
  • Equity and Inclusion: We work to dismantle systemic barriers and ensure everyone—regardless of background—can participate fully in our mission.
  • Collaboration and Solidarity: We build strong alliances with community members, local organizations, and movements to create lasting impact.
  • Transparency and Accountability: We operate openly and honestly, honoring the trust communities place in us and measuring our impact with integrity.
